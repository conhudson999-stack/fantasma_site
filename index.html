Who We Are

TOTAL FOOTBALL.
COMPLETE TRAINING.

Fantasma was born from a deep, unconditional love for the game. Football isn’t just what we do, it’s who we are. That passion drives every session, every drill, every conversation on and off the pitch. Founded by Coach Connor Hudson, a former NCAA Division 1 and semi-professional player, Fantasma exists because the beautiful game deserves to be studied, respected, and pursued with everything you’ve got.

You don’t need to be the fastest, the tallest, or the most athletic player on the field. What you need is a willingness to learn, the ability to think through problems, and a relentless desire to get better every single day. We believe the players who outwork everyone else, the ones who show up early, stay late, and refuse to settle, are the ones who change the game.
