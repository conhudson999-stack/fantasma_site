WHAT A SESSION LOOKS LIKE.

Every session is tailored to the player's goals and current ability. Here's the general flow:

WARM-UP & ACTIVATION

Dynamic movement, ball mastery, and coordination work to get the body and mind locked in.

TECHNICAL DEVELOPMENT

Focused drills on passing, first touch, dribbling, or position-specific skills based on the player's development plan.

FINISHING

Repetitions in front of goal, working on shot placement, composure, and converting chances under pressure.

COOL-DOWN & DEBRIEF

Recovery, review of what we worked on, and key takeaways to practice before the next session.
